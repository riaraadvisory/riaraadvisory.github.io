What We Do,
and What You Get

RIARA Advisory focuses where it matters most — the Marketing, Finance, and Technology decisions that define whether a business grows, stalls, or breaks through. We do not offer everything. We offer the right things, executed with depth and precision, by people who have done this work at the highest level.

Marketing Consulting

Get Found. Get Chosen.
Get Growing.

For businesses that know they have something worth selling — and need the strategy to prove it.

Most small and medium businesses have a marketing problem they cannot quite name. They are active — posting on social media, running occasional ads, maintaining a website — but the results do not match the effort. The issue is rarely execution. It is usually the absence of a clear, grounded strategy sitting underneath the activity.

RIARA's Marketing Consulting practice starts where the answers actually live: in your market, your customers, and the competitive landscape you are operating in. Our advisors have built and scaled marketing functions inside global organizations, led brand repositioning initiatives, and developed go-to-market strategies across multiple sectors and geographies. They bring that experience to bear on your specific challenge — not a template, but a strategy built for your business.

Whether you are entering a new market, reintroducing your brand, or trying to build a more predictable pipeline, we develop the strategic foundation that makes your marketing investment work harder and deliver further.

Typical Deliverables
Brand Audit & Positioning Document
Go-to-Market Strategy
Competitive Landscape Analysis
Digital Presence Assessment
Content Strategy & Editorial Framework
Campaign Planning & Measurement Framework
After Working With RIARA on Marketing, You Will Have:

A clear brand position that differentiates you in your market and gives your team a consistent story to tell across every channel.

A go-to-market strategy built on data and real customer insight — not guesswork or best practices recycled from a different industry.

A digital marketing framework that generates qualified leads with consistency, rather than peaks and valleys tied to ad-hoc campaigns.

A content and communications strategy your internal team can execute — because the best strategy is one your organization can actually carry forward.

Clarity on where your marketing spend is working and where it is not — with a measurement framework that ties activity directly to business outcomes.

Finance Consulting

See Clearly. Plan Confidently.
Grow Sustainably.

For leaders who want to stop managing their finances reactively and start using them as a strategic tool.

Financial clarity is one of the most powerful competitive advantages a growing business can have. When you know exactly where your money is going, what it is producing, and how your numbers map to your strategy, every major decision becomes faster and more grounded. Most small and medium businesses are not there yet — not because they lack ambition, but because the financial infrastructure they need has never been built for them the way it has been built for larger companies.

Our Finance Consulting advisors bring experience from inside corporate finance, investment banking, and executive roles at growth-stage companies. They understand what investor-grade financial models look like, what lenders and capital partners actually scrutinize, and how to translate a business's strategic vision into numbers that tell a credible, compelling story.

Whether you are pursuing your first institutional raise, preparing for a sale, or simply trying to understand why growth is not translating into cash, RIARA brings the financial architecture and clear thinking your business needs to move forward with confidence.

Typical Deliverables
Financial Health Assessment
Custom Financial Model & Projections
Investor Deck & Pitch Preparation
Cash Flow Optimization Plan
KPI Dashboard Design
Capital Structure Advisory
After Working With RIARA on Finance, You Will Have:

Full financial clarity — a granular, honest picture of where your money is going, what it is producing, and where the gaps are costing you growth.

A financial model that connects your strategy to your numbers — so you can see what achieving your goals actually requires, and plan accordingly.

Investor-ready materials if you are pursuing capital — a model and narrative that meet the standard of sophisticated capital partners, not just the standard of looking professional.

An optimized cash flow and margin structure — with specific, actionable changes that improve your financial position without disrupting operations.

A KPI framework that keeps leadership aligned on what matters — so the monthly conversation about performance is focused and forward-looking.

Technology Consulting

Build Smarter. Move Faster.
Scale With Confidence.

For businesses that know technology should be a growth driver — and want to make sure it actually is.

Technology is one of the most significant levers available to a growing business — and one of the most commonly misapplied. The issue is rarely a shortage of available tools. It is the absence of a clear strategy for which tools to adopt, in what order, and in service of what business objective. The result is a patchwork of disconnected systems that create more friction than they remove.

RIARA's Technology Consulting practice is led by advisors who have overseen enterprise digital transformations, evaluated and implemented technology stacks across multiple industries, and built the business cases that got major initiatives funded and executed. They know the difference between a vendor's pitch and the reality of implementation — and they bring that honesty into every engagement.

We help growing businesses gain a clear-eyed view of their current technology landscape, identify where it is holding them back, and build a prioritized roadmap for change. More importantly, we stay with you through execution — because a roadmap without implementation support is just a document.

Typical Deliverables
Technology Audit & Gap Analysis
Digital Transformation Roadmap
Vendor Evaluation & Selection
Implementation Oversight
Process Automation Planning
Systems Integration Strategy
After Working With RIARA on Technology, You Will Have:

A clear, honest picture of your current technology landscape — what is working, what is holding you back, and what the cost of inaction actually is.

A prioritized digital transformation roadmap — with sequenced next steps that match your capacity, budget, and growth objectives, not an aspirational wish list.

Informed vendor decisions — backed by independent evaluation rather than a vendor's self-interest, with clear criteria tied to your specific requirements.

Operational efficiency gains from targeted automation and system integration — freeing your team from manual work and reducing the risk of human error at scale.

A technology foundation that scales with your business — built with the decisions that enterprise companies make, adapted for the realities of a growing Canadian company.
