About

Built From Experience.
Committed to Canadian Business.

RIARA Advisory was born at the intersection of two of the world's most dynamic business ecosystems — and a firm belief that Canada's entrepreneurs deserve more than what the consulting industry has typically given them.

Our Story

Born in the Field.
Not the Boardroom.

RIARA Advisory was not built in a boardroom. It was built through years of working at the intersection of Asia and North America — navigating markets, executing strategy, and watching closely what actually moves companies forward. The pattern was consistent, regardless of country or industry: the businesses that grew were rarely the ones with the best products or the hardest-working teams alone. They were the ones with access to the right strategic thinking at exactly the right moment.

That advantage — elite, calibrated expertise applied with precision — was reliably available to large corporations. For small and medium businesses, it was effectively out of reach. Locked inside expensive consulting practices, structured for clients with enterprise budgets and enterprise timelines. The entrepreneurs who needed it most had the least access to it.

RIARA Advisory was built to change that. Not by diluting the quality of the thinking, but by rethinking how that quality gets delivered — efficiently, precisely, and at a price point that makes sense for growing businesses. Strategy to execution is not just a tagline. It is the commitment that sits behind every engagement we take on.

"Every Canadian business deserves the kind of strategic thinking that was previously reserved for the Fortune 500. That gap is what RIARA was built to close — and the reason we show up to every engagement the way we do."
— RIARA Advisory, Burlington, Ontario
Why Canada

The Engine of This Economy
Deserves Better.

Canada's small and medium businesses employ more than two-thirds of the country's private sector workforce. They anchor communities, create opportunity, and represent some of the most resilient and ambitious entrepreneurship in the world. And yet the advisory services available to them have rarely matched that ambition.

The large consulting firms are structured for enterprise clients — their engagement models, pricing, and staffing are designed for Fortune 500 buyers, not for a 50-person company in Burlington or a 200-person operation in Hamilton. The independent advisors are often well-intentioned but thin on depth, stretched across disciplines they have only read about rather than lived inside.

RIARA exists in that gap by design. Burlington, Ontario is home. But our work spans the country — and the conviction behind it is simple: if you are building something in Canada, you deserve the same quality of strategic support that your largest competitors have access to.

Our Commitment to Canadian SMBs

We do not take on every client who approaches us. We take on the engagements where we are confident we can make a real difference — and we say so clearly when we are not the right fit. That honesty is part of how we operate. Canadian business owners deserve straight answers, not vague assurances and a scope that grows to fill the budget. Every engagement at RIARA is scoped around outcomes, not hours.

The Team

People Who Have Done the Work

The advisors who work with RIARA bring something rare in the consulting world: they have actually done the work at the highest level. Our team includes professionals who have spent careers inside Fortune 500 organizations across Marketing, Finance, and Technology — leading functions, managing P&Ls, structuring deals, and delivering the kind of outcomes that get reported in annual results, not just consulting reports.

Every member of the RIARA advisory team is an alumnus of a leading global MBA program — not because credentials define capability, but because that shared standard of rigor and analytical discipline shows up in how we approach problems. We have sat in the rooms where the decisions that shape industries get made. We know what good strategy looks like under pressure, with real stakes attached.

That depth of experience is what we bring to Canadian small and medium businesses. Not the recycled frameworks of someone who has advised on your industry but never worked in it — the hard-won understanding of someone who has lived inside it.

Our Network

The Right Expert.
Not Just Any Expert.

What makes RIARA different is not just who is on the core team — it is who we can bring to the table. Behind every RIARA engagement sits a curated global network of specialists, built through over a decade of professional relationships across Asia and North America.

Marketing Specialists

From performance marketing architects with deep e-commerce expertise to brand strategists who have repositioned global companies, our marketing network spans the full discipline. When your challenge demands a niche, we activate the right specialist — not the nearest available generalist.

Finance Specialists

Technology Specialists

This network is RIARA's structural advantage. It means every client, regardless of budget or scope, has access to expertise that was previously out of reach. The right person, brought in precisely — that is the RIARA difference.
